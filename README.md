# Privacy Policy for “Cat Climbing | Wall Jump Adventures”

Last updated: 21.11.2025
Privacy Policy for CatClimbing game 

This Privacy Policy explains how Very Soft Soft collects and uses information when you play the mobile game “Cat Climbing | Wall Jump Adventures”. By installing or using the game, you agree to the terms described here.

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, address, or phone number. The game uses third-party services that may automatically collect certain non-personal data. This information is used to improve the quality of the game, fix technical issues, and provide advertising.

The game uses Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, Google AdMob and default Unity services. These services may collect information such as device type, operating system version, crash data, performance data, region based on IP address, advertising ID and interactions with ads. This information is processed according to the respective privacy policies of Google, Firebase, AdMob and Unity. We do not store or manage this data ourselves.

The information collected through third-party services is used to analyze gameplay behavior, improve performance, identify and fix crashes, ensure proper functioning of the game and display ads. We do not sell user data to third parties.

The game is intended for a general audience and does not contain violent or harmful content. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. Parents who believe that their child may have shared data through third-party services may contact us.

The game relies on third-party providers including Google Firebase, Google AdMob and Unity Technologies. Their privacy policies describe how they process data. Users may refer to these policies for detailed information.

The game may include optional in-app purchases in the future. All payments will be processed through the official stores such as Google Play or the App Store. We do not handle or store any payment information.

We do not operate our own servers. Data security is handled by the trusted third-party platforms that process the information.

By using the game, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. We may update this document from time to time. Any changes will appear on the public privacy policy page.
